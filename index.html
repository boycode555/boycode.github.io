Breaking the Barrier to Tech

Making Tech Inclusive
and Accessible

We are dedicated to breaking down the barriers that often hinder
individuals from accessing opportunities in the tech industry.
Understanding the challenges faced by aspiring tech enthusiasts, We
actively work to eliminate obstacles and create a level playing field.

By providing accessible training programs, mentorship, and a supportive
community, We ensure that no one is left behind due to financial
constraints, lack of resources, or limited networks.

Through our inclusive approach, We are democratizing tech education
and opening doors for individuals from diverse backgrounds to pursue
their passion and thrive in the tech field.

Training Programs

Entry level and intermediate
training and up-skill programs

01

Zuri Training

We understand a complete beginner cannot
become a professional after a few weeks, the
aim here is to get you familiar enough with each
the tools such that you'll be able to continue
learning on your own with minimal support.

02

Zuri Internship

We place you in a work simulation. We give you
tasks every week, the tasks ranges from easy
to hard as you progress from stage to stage,
making it to the final stage means you are
ready for actual work.

03

Zuri Continuous Development Training
(CDT)

At Zuri CDT, We are dedicated to helping
individuals sharpen their skills and elevate their
careers through continuous development.
